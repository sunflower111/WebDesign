Some Interesting Things About Sponge Bob Square Pants and Friends!

Sponge Bob and his friends are interesting characters. They each have a unique personality and varied talents. Let's take a look at him and some of his friends!!!

Sponge Bob

Sponge Bob is everyone's friend. He enjoys jelly fishing and hanging out with his friends. He has been known to make music with a tissue and make pickles squeak. He can even talk snail. If all this isn't interesting enough, he lives in a pineapple under the sea!

Gary

Gary is Sponge Bob's pet snail. He meows and wear's shoes. Enough said about Gary.

Patrick Star

Patrick is a pink starfish. He wears flowered shorts and doesn't have any feet. He lives under a rock and is Sponge Bob's best friend.

Squidward

Squidward, another one of Sponge Bob's friends, enjoys playing the clarinet. He is usually in a dismal mood, but still a fun character.

Mr. Crabs

Mr. Crabs is Sponge Bob's Boss. He owns the Crusty Crab. He still has the first dollar he ever made.
