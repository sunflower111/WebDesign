AP 2D Design Summer Homework

Bright Summer Day

This is part of my summer homework for AP 2D Design. It was part of a study of how different lighting changes the atmosphere of an art piece. This one has the lighting of a sunny day, which makes it cheerful, but peaceful. The hard lighting makes the scene seem brighter.

Rainy Summer Day

This is another part of my summer homework for AP 2D Design. It was part of the same study of how different lighting changes the atmosphere of an art piece. This one has the lighting of a rainy day, which makes it muted, but peaceful. The soft lighting makes it more muted.
