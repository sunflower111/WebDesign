The Acropolis

Located in Athens, the capital city of Greece, you will find the Acropolis. It's home to many beautiful examples of architecture including Doric columns, spectacular temples, and the Parthenon.

Mount Olympus

If you enjoy hiking or mountain climbing, Mount Olympus is a great travel destination for you! If you are a fan of Greek Mythology, this is a great place to visit too! In Greek Mythology, it is the home of the gods.

Melissani Cave

Come to Karavomilo to visit the Melissani Cave! Inside this beautiful 4 kilimeter long cave is the Melissani Lake. It's an underground body of water that was named after the Nymph Melissani from ancient times.

You enter the cave through a tunnel and travel across it on a boat.
