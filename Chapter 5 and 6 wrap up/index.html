Foxes

The information on foxes includes red foxes and gray foxes. Both of these foxes can be found in forests. Another aspect that they have in common is that neither types of foxes chew their food. Instead, they swallow small prey whole or rip off small pieces. All foxes walk on their toes, which is called digitigrade. Click the image or here for more information.
